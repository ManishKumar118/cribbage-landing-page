How it Play?

How to Play Cribbage with Cribbage Pegboard

This concise summary should help users quickly grasp how to use the app alongside their Cribbage game. Let me know if you'd like to tweak this further!

Set Up the Game

Use your own physical cards to play the game while the app acts as a digital pegboard to keep score. It supports both 2-player and 3-player configurations.

Track Your Scores

Drag and drop pegs to record your scores, or let the app handle automatic scoring as you play your hands and peg points. The app keeps a visual display of the board so you can focus on your strategy.

Strategize Using Discard Analyzer

Use the Discard Analyzer to get insights into your discards. It calculates the minimum, maximum, and average score for each possible discard, helping you improve your chances to win.
